Quick guidance
Use these steps to move smoothly from dashboard to inputs and then to the new results page.

How to run a prediction

  1. Open a module from the dashboard.
  2. Review the auto-filled values pulled from your latest health data.
  3. Edit any input you want. Helper text updates in real time while typing.
  4. Submit the form to move to the results page.
  5. Review the chart, color-matched breakdown bars, and recommendations.

Display improvements

  • Screen-based durations are previewed as human-readable hour/minute text in the UI.
  • Floating labels keep the forms compact and responsive.
  • The Help and Contact actions now sit in a movable corner utility area controlled by CSS.
